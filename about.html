About Us

At Wan-Puck Upholstery, we’re passionate about turning your furniture into timeless pieces you’ll treasure. Based in Midrand, Gauteng, our team brings unmatched skill and attention to detail in every project — from classic couch restorations to bold custom creations. We pride ourselves on quality craftsmanship, elegant design, and making your vision a reality.


We breath new life into your beloved furniture with expert craftsmanship and a wide range of stylish fabrics. Here we restore comfort, beauty, and value - one piece at a time.
